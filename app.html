About Us

Soni Electricals & Engineering Works is a precision manufacturing company specializing in CNC and conventional machining services. Located in Kharwai, District Raisen on the Main Road Raisen–Bhopal, we serve industries requiring high-quality custom components and engineering solutions.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a commitment to excellence, we transform raw materials into precision-engineered components that meet the most demanding specifications. Our expertise spans across various materials including Copper, MS, SS, Aluminium, and Plastics.
